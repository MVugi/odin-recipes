Fries Recipe

Name

Description

More Description

Ingredients

  • 3 kg of potatoes
  • Knife
  • Fryer
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop potatoes into thin sticks (leave peel if you prefer a rustic taste)
  2. Deep fry potato sticks
  3. Toss in Salt
  4. Drop the golden brown fries on the floor and shed a tear
